A few playables by Arpita for Kwalee

Build Your Vehicle: Decision Gates

One must build their vehicle as the terrain changes. There are two gates to choose from and four competitors. Who will win?

Landlord Simulator: Inspection

The tenant seems to do suspicious activity. Help the landlord to inspect the house and decide whether to renew the Tenant's contract or not.

Dream Build Soltaire: Granny Cook

Help save Granny's house by choose suitable cards and extinguish the fire.

Line Up: Doodling the Criminal

There is a witness helping with picturing the criminal. Follow the description by them and sketch the criminal. Also help find the criminal amongst 3 suspects.

Perfect Coffee: Drink Maker

Pour the coffee, milk and foam. Choose an art to decorate the coffee with. Carefuly sprinkle the powder and make your drink!

Airport Security: Both dogs

Dogs are going through security at an airport. Dont fall for the cuteness. They might carry harmfull items.

Airport Security: Husky (short version)

The above playable, but just Husky here. This playable was made to cut the gameplay shorter.

Airport Security: Husky (short version)

Same as above playable but just Shiba here. In a similar manner, other playables also had shorter and one click versions.

Perfect Coffee: Merge Mechanic

This playable is a work on progress. Merge two drinks to serve the customer.

Landlord Simulator: Interview

Help the landlord to choose tenants with least issues.
